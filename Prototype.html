Donor Eligibility Criteria

Age Requirement

Donors must be between 18 and 65 years old. Donors above 65 may be accepted at the doctor's discretion.

Weight Requirement

Donors must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs). This ensures your safety during the donation process.

Health Condition

Donors must be in good general health and feeling well on the day of donation. No fever or infections.

Donation Frequency

Whole blood can be donated every 56 days. Platelet donations can be made every 7 days, up to 24 times per year.

Medication

Most medications are acceptable. Blood thinners may require a waiting period. Discuss any medications with our staff.

Travel History

Recent travel to certain countries may temporarily prevent you from donating. This is to prevent disease transmission.

Medical Procedures

If you've had surgery, you may need to wait before donating. Typically a 6-month wait after major surgery is required.

Tattoos & Piercings

You must wait 3-12 months after getting a tattoo or piercing, depending on local regulations and sterilization practices.

About LifeLink

LifeLink is a platform dedicated to connecting blood donors with those in need. Our mission is to make the process of finding blood donors efficient, quick, and reliable.

We work with hospitals, blood banks, and individual donors to create a network that ensures blood is available when and where it's needed most.

Our Vision

To create a world where no one dies waiting for a blood transfusion.
